# SatSplit AI — AlphaTON Capital Pitch Script

**Time budget: 5 minutes total. Demo: 90 seconds. Talk: 3.5 minutes.**

---

## Opening (20 seconds)

"Your Telegram group just had dinner. Someone paid. Now the uncomfortable silence begins."

"The group chat goes quiet. Nobody wants to be the one to ask. And when they do — three people say they already sent it, nobody can verify, and the conversation dies."

"SatSplit fixes this inside Telegram — no new app, no wallets to share, no awkward messages."

---

## The problem (30 seconds)

"Splitting bills in a group has three problems:"

1. "The math — nobody wants to calculate 7 people's shares of a 43-item receipt"
2. "The collection — you have to chase people individually, out of band"
3. "The trust — you never know for sure who paid until you check your bank app"

"Existing solutions like Splitwise or Tricount require everyone to download an app, create an account, and connect bank details. For a one-time friend group dinner, nobody does that."

---

## The solution (30 seconds)

"SatSplit lives inside Telegram. Zero install. Here's how it works:"

→ **START DEMO**

1. Photo of the receipt → bot responds in 4 seconds with full breakdown
2. "I correct one item — the bot got the tax wrong — one tap"
3. "I assign Alice, Bob, and Charlie. Alice uses her ENS name, the other two have Telegram handles"
4. "Three payment links go out. Each person taps, Tonkeeper opens, they send TON"
5. "Status board updates in real time. Green, green, pending. I tap 'remind Charlie' — bot sends him a nudge"

→ **END DEMO — 90 seconds**

---

## Why TON (30 seconds)

"We chose TON because it's where the users already are. Every Telegram user is one tap away from Tonkeeper. We're not asking anyone to leave their existing app."

"And the viral loop is built in — every split creates 3-5 new users who see SatSplit for the first time when they get a payment request."

---

## Monetization (20 seconds)

"Free for consumers. The x402 premium tier unlocks AI parsing for long receipts — 20+ items, handwritten, multi-currency — for 0.01 TON per parse. That's the revenue model."

"For assos, group organizers, and small merchants who run regular events — we'll offer a pro plan with history, reminders, and analytics."

---

## Closing (10 seconds)

"Not a crypto app on Telegram. A Telegram app that uses TON."

"SatSplit."

---

## Judge Q&A prep

**Q: How do you get users?**
A: Every payment request is a distribution event. The recipient doesn't need SatSplit installed — they just tap the Tonkeeper link. After they pay, the bot says "Create your own split" with a direct link.

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: x402 micro-payments for premium AI (consumer tier) + pro subscription for recurring organizers (assos, group chats, small merchants).

**Q: What's your path to 1,000 users?**
A: Target EPFL and university student groups in Telegram. One organizer per group = 10-20 new exposed users per split. Campus assos that run weekly events are the wedge.
